Compliance Standards

We maintain the highest standards of legal and ethical compliance in the adult industry

18+ Age Verification

All content is age-gated and restricted to adults 18 years and older. We enforce strict age verification requirements across our entire network.

2257 Compliance

Full adherence to 18 U.S.C. § 2257 record-keeping requirements for all adult content distributed through our platform.

GDPR & Privacy

Full compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations. User privacy and data security are paramount.

Content Review

Every campaign, creative, and landing page is manually reviewed by our compliance team before going live.

Partner Vetting

Thorough background checks and compliance reviews for all new advertisers, publishers, and affiliates.

Payment Compliance

KYC/AML compliance for all financial transactions. We work with licensed payment processors and maintain full audit trails.

Zero Tolerance Policy: We have zero tolerance for illegal content, trafficking, non-consensual material, or underage content of any kind. Violations result in immediate termination and reporting to authorities.

Partner Review Process

How we ensure quality and compliance across our network

1

Application Submission

Partners submit applications with business details, traffic sources or inventory, and compliance documentation.

2

Initial Review

Our team reviews basic eligibility: business type, traffic quality indicators, and preliminary compliance check.

3

Compliance Verification

Deep dive into compliance: content review, age verification practices, legal documentation, and background checks.

4

Quality Assessment

For publishers: traffic quality analysis. For advertisers: offer and creative review. For affiliates: traffic source validation.

5

Approval & Onboarding

Approved partners receive access credentials, account setup, and onboarding support to get started.

⏱️ Review Timeline: Most applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days.
